What we sell

Our stock spans the full spectrum of world paper money, with particular depth in areas that are hard to source elsewhere.

Specimen Notes

Perforated and overprinted printer's specimens — never intended for circulation, issued in controlled quantities.

Overprints & Surcharges

Wartime and transitional overprints, including the complete Bosnia UNCIVPOL Sarajevo series.

Hyperinflation Notes

Zimbabwe trillion-dollar denominations, Weimar-era Germany, Yugoslavia, and other historic inflation episodes.

Error Notes

Production errors that reached circulation — missing serials, double prints, inverted overprints.

African Issues

Sub-Saharan and North African notes, including colonial-era types and scarce inaugural central bank issues.

High-Grade UNC

Uncirculated examples across all regions, from common series to hard-to-find survivors in gem condition.

Why collectors choose MM·Money

We are active collectors ourselves, not warehouse resellers. Every note passes through our hands, is evaluated against catalogue references, and is listed with an honest grade and clear photographs — front and reverse. If a note has a flaw, we say so. If it is exceptional, we explain why.

Our feedback record on both Delcampe and eBay reflects years of reliable transactions with collectors across Europe, the Americas, Asia, and beyond. Shipping is tracked, packaged to numismatic standards, and insured on request.
